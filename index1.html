Maricopa Accidents VS Weather Conditions 2020

The purpose of this project is to analyze the accidents in 2020 for Maricopa county and compare with weather conditions. We will review different variables of weather such as, Season, Temperarture, Precipitation, Windspeed, at the time of the incidents. We will also observe the total accidents for each varaible to try to confirm if weather did increase or decrease accidents incidents.
