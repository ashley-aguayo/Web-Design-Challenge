Accidents VS Daily Temperature



This graph above shows the total number of accidents per day with the daily average temperature.

This graph shows the daily average temperature vs the daily accident count. It also shows a linear regression for the data preseneted. This also shows that as the temperatures increased the daily accidents actually decreased. It may have been due to fewer vehicles on the road during summmer, when the pandemic stay at home orders were enforced.
