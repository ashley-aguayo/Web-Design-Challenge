Weather Conditions VS Accident Severity



This graph above shows the severity of each accident and the weather conditions.

We explored this topic to dig in a little deeper for the different accidents. We know how many accidents occured for the seasons, but wanted to know if specific weather conditions effected the severity of the accident. The lower the number the less severe. We can see there is not a huge variance amongst the conditions, however Fair, Haze and Cloudy did have the highest amounts of most severe accidents. .
