Accidents Per Season



This graph above shows the total number of accidents per each season in Maricopa County in 2020.

We predicted that winter would produce higher accidents with drops in temperatures, however we can see that is not what our data shows. We can see that spring had the highest rate of accidents. We were wrong. In second place comes summer, we may guess the higher temperatures may have effected the incidents. Next we see autumn in third place just about equal with summer. Lastly winter, which had the lowest amount of total accidents recorded. We may guess that since Maricopa does not have extreme weather conditions, winter may acutally create more favorable driving conditions in Maricopa County.
